Welcome to the Midland Wheeler's Cycling Club

What we do

Established in 2021 The Midlands Cycling Club is based in Athlone, Co. Westmeath. We are a group of enthusiasts from all walks of life and ability levels, who meet regularly to enjoy the fun and healthy sport of cycling.

We meet on Saturday mornings at 10:00am (weather permitting), at the Old Rail Trail entrance at the White Gates, and proceed along the newly-established Greenway. This linear route provides for varying distances, thereby accomodating cyclists of all levels and abilities.

Our Saturday ride is followed by an opportunity to socialise and enjoy coffee at the RSC, which conveniently has a trail linking it to the greenway. The RSC has it's own secure area for bike parking and we enjoy a discounted group price.

Come and Join Us

New members are always welcome. Our members come from all walks of life, but share one thing in common, a love of outdoor, social, and environmentally-friendly activity. If you wish to keep fit, make new friends and enjoy leisurely activities, you'll feel right at home with us.
